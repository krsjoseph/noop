Decision Notes

If you want the safest move

Use Kineva Fit as the near-term public name. It has the cleanest verified availability path and preserves the existing community, repo recognition, subreddit, and mirror story.

If you want the strongest long-term consumer brand

Move toward Kineva. It is less tied to the current interoperability story and better suited to a future with built-in workouts, demo videos, form coaching, and subscription-level polish.

Names I would avoid

Do not lead with Strand as the public brand. It is meaningful internally, but too common and not clearly fitness. I would also avoid anything directly tied to WHOOP, "strap", or "recovery only" because the future product scope is broader.

Pre-launch checklist

Buy the target domain, reserve exact and variant handles, run USPTO/WIPO/App Store/Play Store searches, test pronunciation with five people, and mock one App Store screenshot before committing to the rename.
